    How do I set the time?

    This is done automatically after the unit is plugged in.


    How do I set up a feeding schedule?

    Click on the "Feed" tab above. Choose the time you want the unit to release food and the amount you want it to release. Click on "Disabled" and change it to "Enabled". Click "Submit" and the changes to the feeding schedule will be saved to the unit.


    How do I set up a second feeding schedule?

    At the moment we don't have the capability for that.


    How much food will the unit hold?

    The hopper unit will hold a full 10 pounds of dry pet food.


    Do I have to fill the unit all the way?

    No. You can fill it as full or empty as you desire.


    How do I clean the bowl?

    The bowl is easily removed from the unit so it can be washed. It's even dishwasher safe!


    How do I set the unit to text/email me when it is nearly or completely empty?

    We can't do email just yet.

    Text messages are set up on the "Feed" page. Fill in your text message capable phone number in the spaces next to "SMS number", click "Submit" and the unit will notify you if there are any problems.


    What size kibble should I use?

    We recommend a medium size kibble, though the unit will operate with both large and small sizes.


    What size dog is this product recommended for?

    Medium (like Terriers) to Large (like St. Bernard) dogs.


    How do I activate the RFID?

    This is a future feature of our product. If you own version 1 then you don't have an RFID card and will need to buy version 2 when it comes out.


    Is the RFID safe?

    Yes. We've done lots of safety tests to make sure of that.


    How do I activate/deactivate the Dinner Bell?

    This feature is coming soon.

    Click on the "Dinner Bell" tab above. Click on "Disabled" and change it to "Enabled". Click "Submit" and the tone will sound when the unit next dispenses food.


    Can I change the Dinner Bell sound?

    Certainly. Find the sound file you want to use on your computer. Go to the "Dinner Bell" tab and where it says "Add sound file:" type in the file path of the sound file. Click "Submit". The alternate sound file has been saved to your unit and will sound when the unit next dispenses food.


    What is My Pet Watch?

    My Pet Watch is a camera mounted on the front of the unit that will let you see who is eating from the bowl. The camera is angled so that you can see your pet up to three feet in front of the machine.


    How do I access My Pet Watch?

    This is a future feature of our product. If you own version 1 then you don't have an RFID card and will need to buy version 2 when it comes out.


    Can others access My Pet Watch?

    No. The camera must be manually turned on at the unit and the visual feed activated by a password provided by you on startup.


    How does the Feed Now button work?

    It will release a pre-set 1 cup amount of food into the bowl any time the button is pressed. This button is good for testing the unit when you first get it and feeding your pet immediately after a power outage.
